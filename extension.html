PUP Lopez Extension Program

KITA MOTO: Gabay-Negosyo Pangkababaihan

A livelihood and entrepreneurship extension project of the Bachelor of Science in Business Administration Major in Marketing Management focused on empowering women through practical product-making workshops and sustainable community livelihood programs.

Women Empowerment Through Livelihood

Conducted at Barangay Villahermosa, Lopez, Quezon, the extension program provides hands-on entrepreneurial training activities for women in the community.

Program Phases

Extension Program Activities

The KITA MOTO extension program is divided into livelihood training phases that provide participants with practical entrepreneurial and product-making skills.

PHASE 1

Dishwashing Liquid Making

The first phase focused on seminar-training workshops for dishwashing liquid production. Participants learned proper mixing procedures, preparation of materials, packaging, and basic product costing for livelihood purposes.

PHASE 2

Fabric Conditioner Making

The second phase introduced participants to fabric conditioner production and entrepreneurship strategies. The activity highlighted product formulation, fragrance preparation, and small-scale business opportunities.
